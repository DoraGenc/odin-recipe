☆ BRUSCHETTA ☆

Let's get started with making bruschetta!

Ingredients:

  • a Baguette
  • 5 Roma Tomatoes
  • Basil leaves
  • 3-4 cloves of Garlic
  • Parmesan
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper

step-by-step instructions

1. Prepare the bread
  1. Slice your baguette into thin slices.
  2. Finely mince some garlic and leave about 1-2 cloves for the topping.
  3. Infuse oil with minced garlic. Then brush it onto both sides of the bread.
  4. Then sprinkle some parmesan on top of the slices.
  5. Put your slices onto a sheet of parchment paper and bake them at about 200°C until they are golden brown.
    6. During this time, begin with preparing the topping.
2. Prepare the topping
  1. Core and dice Tomatoes and drain the excess juice. Put the tomatoes into a bowl.
  2. Stack the basil leaves, roll them into a tube and thinly slice your rolled basil leaves and finally add them to your bowl. Leave some for later.
  3. Also, add the rest of your minced garlic.
  4. Season everything with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar and a bit of salt & pepper.
  5. Stir gently and leave it aside to marinate for 20-30 minutes.
3. Make the Bruschetta Toasts
  1. Take your bread out of the oven, wait for them to cool off.
  2. Now distribute the topping onto the slices.
  3. Finally, garnish your Bruschetta with the rest of the fresh basil and drizzle them with balsamic vinegar and olive oil.

Enjoy!!
