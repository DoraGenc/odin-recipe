☆ LEMON TARTE ☆

Best for last: Lemon Tarte!!

Ingredients:

for the crust:
  • 175g almond flour
  • 120g wheat flour
  • 100g sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 115g softened butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 large egg

for the filling:

  • 1 glass of lemon curd
  • additionally: whipped cream & lemon zest

step-by-step instructions

1. Prepare & bake the crust
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C using the convection setting.
  2. In a big bowl, mix the almond and wheat flour, sugar and salt.
  3. Add the softened butter, as well as the vanilla and the egg, then mix.
  4. Seperate the dough into 6 pieces, and put the rust evenly into small buttered pans.
  5. Then bake them for about 15-20mins until golden brown.
  6. Let them cool off before adding the filling.
2. Filling
  1. Fill your cool crusts with lemon curd.
  2. Additionally, add some whipped cream on top with a piping bag and sprinkle some lemon zest!
