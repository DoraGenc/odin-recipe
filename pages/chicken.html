☆ HONEY GARLIC CHICKEN ☆

Now the main course: honey garlic chicken!

Ingredients:

  • 600g chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • sunflower oil
  • Butter
  • 2-3 cloves of Garlic
  • 150-250g honey
  • 2 tablespoons Vegetable stock powder
  • 2 tablespoons Gochujang pepper paste
  • 1 tablespoon Miso paste
  • 50ml Soy sauce
  • Salt & Pepper
  • some finely chopped parsley
  • boiled rice
  • cooked broccoli

step-by-step instructions

1. Prepare the Sauce
  1. Take a bowl and put in the honey, soy sauce, Gochujang pepper paste, Miso paste, Vegetable stock powder and some pepper and salt.
  2. Then add some hot water to the bowl and stir. It shouldn´t be completely smooth. Keep in mind that some water will evaporate.
2. Prepare & fry the chicken breast
  1. Slice your chicken breast into thin slices.
  2. Coast the chicken the he cornstarch, as well as pepper and salt.
  3. Finely mince the garlic.
  4. Heat up some oil in a pan. When the oil is hot, add the chicken. When one side is golden brown, flip it and add your minced garlic.
  5. When your chicken is ready, take it out of the pan.
3. Cook the Sauce
  1. Add your mixture to the hot pan.
  2. Stir it until it cooks and becomes thicker.
  3. When it is thick enough, take off the heat and add the chicken.
4. Serve
  1. Serve the Chicken and the sauce on boiled rice with broccoli!

Enjoy :3
